🏦 ComplyAI – AI Governance Sandbox for Banks

🚀 Overview

ComplyAI is an AI Governance Sandbox platform designed for banks and financial institutions to test, validate, and ensure compliance of AI models before deploying them in real-world scenarios.

As AI adoption in banking grows, models like loan approval and fraud detection often act as black boxes, creating risks of bias, unfair decisions, and regulatory violations.
ComplyAI solves this by bringing transparency, explainability, and compliance checks into one unified platform.

---

🎯 Problem Statement

- AI models in banking lack transparency
- Risk of bias and unfair loan approvals
- Strict regulations require auditability and explainability
- Non-compliance can lead to heavy penalties and trust issues

---

💡 Our Solution

ComplyAI provides a sandbox where banks can:

- Upload AI models
- Test them against compliance rules
- Detect bias and fairness issues
- Generate explainable outputs
- Ensure regulatory alignment before deployment

---

⚙️ Key Features

- 🔍 Model Upload & Testing
- ⚖️ Bias & Fairness Detection
- 📊 Explainable AI (XAI) Reports
- 🛡️ Compliance Validation

- 🎨 (In Progress) Improved UI/UX

---

🧩 System Architecture

1. Frontend (User Interface)
   
   - Built using Streamlit / Web UI
   - Allows users to upload models and view reports

2. Backend (API Layer)
   
   - Built using FastAPI
   - Handles model processing, validation, and analysis

3. AI Engine
   
   - Performs bias detection and explainability analysis

4. Compliance Engine
   
   - Applies banking rules and regulatory checks

---

🔄 Workflow

1. Bank uploads AI model
2. Model is analyzed for:
   - Bias
   - Fairness
   - Transparency
3. Compliance engine validates against regulations
4. System generates:
   - Reports
   - Risk flags
   - Recommendations
